Process of producing Rubber Sheets

Our pimary goal is of producing high quality latex from rubber trees and transforming them into rubber sheets for suppliers and manufacturers.

How to Transfom Latex into Rubber Sheet

  • Each night a new cut is made causing a new flow of latex.
  • Latex flow begins.
  • Latex flows toward a collection cup.
  • Latex begins to flow into the cup.
  • Latex is then coagulated in a pan.
  • Water is rolled out of the cake of fresh rubber.
  • Fresh sheets of raw rubber are dried and made read for supply.
