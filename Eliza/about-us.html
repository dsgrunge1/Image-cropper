Introduction

Welcome to Eliza Rubber Plantations, residing in a place with a potential of a booming rubber industry. We are an organisation solely dedicated to the interests of the northeastern region of India. Currently we are situated in the Udalguri district of Assam, India. Here in this region of our country, we are trying to harness the rich ingredients of nature for the purpose producing high quality rubber. Northeast has always been blessed with the gifts of natural resources, culture and residence of different ethnic tribes. But, it is still lagging behind with a poor financial economy. This is why we thrive to introduce employment, education and health care through our organisation. Our contribution towards the people of our region is also to raise the quality of life which has been disrupted by the absence of basic human needs.

Our organisation is on the verge of starting the poduction of rubber soon for suppliers and manufacturers...

Our Future Endeavors

  • Supplier
  • Manufacturer
  • Training
Being a supplier would be very beneficial both for the end-users and us. Our output product would be supplied by our team directly to the end-users. This in return will reduce the supply-chain which always increases the cost of final product.
We will be manufacturing various rubber products which are very essential in farming and agriculture.
Training is very important and necessary for every organisation. That is why we are going provide various taining progams to educate and guide people about the constituents and applications of rubber in our lives.
